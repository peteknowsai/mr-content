# Events — SW Florida

Write event and tournament cards for SW Florida captains covering boat shows, fishing tournaments, and boating happenings.

## Annual Fishing Tournaments

### Tarpon Tournaments (May-July)
- **Boca Grande Tarpon Tournament** — Historic tournament, catch-and-release
- **World's Richest Tarpon Tournament** — Boca Grande, significant purse
- **Tarpon Time in Paradise** — Marathon/Key Largo, many SW FL participants
- **Captiva Island Tarpon Tournament** — Family-friendly format
- **Location advantage**: Cape Coral marina closest to Boca Grande Pass

### Offshore Tournaments
- **Southwest Florida Slam** — Multi-species offshore tournament
- **Naples/Marco Island tournaments** — Various clubs host events
- **Kingfish tournaments** — Winter season (Oct-Mar)
- **Cobia tournaments** — Spring migration (Mar-May)
- **Location advantage**: Naples and Marco marinas for Gulf access

### Inshore Tournaments
- **Redfish tournaments** — Charlotte Harbor area (fall peak)
- **Snook tournaments** — When season is open (Sep-May, Dec-Jan)
- **Everglades challenges** — Backcountry events from Marco area
- **Kids' fishing tournaments** — Family events throughout region

### Stone Crab Events (Oct 15 - May 15)
- **Stone Crab Festival** — Everglades City (multiple weekends)
- **Goodland Stone Crab Festival** — Marco Island area
- **Restaurant competitions** — Best stone crab dishes

## Boat Shows & Expos

### Southwest Florida Boat Shows
- **Fort Myers Boat Show** — Annual, typically January/February
- **Naples Boat Show** — Spring event, luxury focus
- **Marco Island events** — Smaller shows, local dealers
- **In-water displays** — Some shows feature water displays

### Specialty Shows  
- **Flats boat expo** — Shallow water boat focus
- **Fishing gear expo** — Tackle and equipment shows  
- **Marine electronics** — Technology showcases
- **Boat accessories** — Equipment and upgrade shows

## Seasonal Events

### Winter Season (Dec-Mar)
- **Stone crab season** opening celebrations
- **Snowbird welcome events** — Marina and club gatherings
- **Holiday boat parades** — Decorated boats, evening events
- **Manatee awareness** events — Education and protection focus
- **Peak tournament season** — Best weather for events

### Spring (Apr-May)
- **Tarpon season** kickoff events
- **Boat shows** — Many held in comfortable spring weather
- **Safety courses** — Coast Guard auxiliary, Power Squadron
- **Marina events** — Season kickoff parties and gatherings

### Summer (Jun-Aug)
- **Early morning events** — Beat the heat and storms
- **Kids' programs** — Junior angler events, boating safety
- **Conservation events** — Manatee, sea turtle awareness
- **Reduced activity** — Many events pause during hot season

### Fall (Sep-Nov)
- **Hurricane season** — Many events cancelled/postponed
- **Fall fishing tournaments** — As weather improves
- **Boat maintenance** — Prep for upcoming season
- **Storm recovery** — Community events if storms hit
