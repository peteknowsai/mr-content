# Dining — SW Florida

Write dock-and-dine and waterfront restaurant cards for SW Florida captains.

## Dock-and-Dine from Naples

### Naples Bay Area
- **The Dock Restaurant** — Crayton Cove. Upscale waterfront dining, good dockage. Naples Bay location
- **Riverwalk Fish & Ale** — Naples Bay. Casual seafood, outdoor seating, live music
- **M Waterfront Grille** — Village on Venetian Bay. Upscale, excellent sunset views
- **Bayside Seafood Grill & Bar** — Walker's Hideaway Marina. Casual, local favorite

### Gordon River Area  
- **Pinchers** — Tin City, Naples. Casual seafood chain, good grouper, shrimp
- **Riverwalk Restaurant** — Historic Tin City location. Classic Florida seafood

## Dock-and-Dine from Marco Island

### Marco River
- **The Snook Inn** — Marco River. Institution since 1976. Tiki bar, live music, grouper sandwich
- **CJ's on the Bay** — Marco Island. Waterfront dining, marina location
- **Marker 8.5** — Goodland. Authentic Old Florida, stone crab in season
- **Little Bar Restaurant** — Goodland. Historic fishing village atmosphere

### Factory Bay/Ten Thousand Islands
- **Capri Fish House** — Capri. Fresh seafood, stone crab specialist  
- **Margood Harbourfront Restaurant** — Goodland. Sunset dining, stone crab

## Dock-and-Dine from Cape Coral

### Caloosahatchee River
- **Rumrunners** — Cape Coral. Popular tiki bar, live music, sunset views
- **Boathouse Tiki Bar** — Cape Coral. Riverfront location, casual dining
- **Deep Lagoon Seafood & Oyster House** — Cape Coral canals access

### Charlotte Harbor Area
- **Fishermen's Village** — Punta Gorda. Multiple restaurants, good dockage, shopping
- **Laishley Crab House** — Punta Gorda. Waterfront fine dining, marina setting
- **Captain's Table Lodge** — Charlotte Harbor. Old Florida charm, fresh local fish

### Pine Island Sound  
- **Waterfront Restaurant** — St. James City, Pine Island. Sunset dining, live music
- **Tarpon Lodge Restaurant** — Pine Island. Historic lodge, waterfront fine dining
- **Perfect Caper** — Punta Gorda. Upscale, marina location

## Sanibel/Captiva Access
- **The Mucky Duck** — Captiva Island. Beach restaurant, sunset views (anchor off beach)
- **'Tween Waters Inn** — Captiva. Historic resort restaurant, dockage available
- **Lazy Flamingo** — Sanibel. Casual beach bar, multiple locations

## Boca Grande Area (Near Cape Coral)
- **The Inlet** — Boca Grande. Upscale dining, marina location
- **Uncle Henry's Marina Restaurant** — Boca Grande. Casual, fishing crowd
- **PJ's Seagrille** — Boca Grande. Fine dining, sunset views

## By Boat Worth the Run

### Everglades Area
- **Rod & Gun Club** — Everglades City. Historic, Old Florida atmosphere
- **Camellia Street Grill** — Everglades City. Casual, stone crab capital

### Outer Islands
- **Cabbage Key Inn** — Historic island restaurant (Cheeseburger in Paradise inspiration)
- **Useppa Inn** — Useppa Island. Upscale historic inn, advance reservations required

## Special Categories

### Stone Crab Season (Oct 15 - May 15)
- Marker 8.5, Margood, Capri Fish House — specialize in fresh stone crab
- Many restaurants feature stone crab during season — SW Florida specialty

### Tiki Bars & Casual
- The Snook Inn, Rumrunners, Boathouse Tiki Bar — laid-back waterfront vibes
- Perfect for post-fishing, sunset cocktails

### Fine Dining
- M Waterfront Grille, Laishley Crab House, Tarpon Lodge — upscale waterfront
- Good for special occasions, date nights

## Card Types
- **Restaurant spotlight** — detailed guide to one restaurant, dock info, specialties, how to approach
- **Dock-and-dine roundup** — best options from specific marina
- **Stone crab trail** — seasonal guide to best stone crab spots
- **Sunset dining** — restaurants with best sunset views accessible by boat
- **After-fishing casual** — quick, easy stops near fishing spots

## Port32 Tie-ins
- Fuel up at marina before dinner runs
- Valet service means easy departure/return for dinner trips  
- Marina concierge can help with restaurant reservations
- Boat cleaning after return from dinner runs

## Research Tips
- Check dock availability and approach depths
- Confirm restaurant hours and seasonal closures  
- Note if reservations recommended/required
- Include approach instructions and hazards
- Mention parking alternatives for crew pickup
