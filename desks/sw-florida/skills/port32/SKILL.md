# Port32 — SW Florida

Write cards that showcase Port32 Naples, Port32 Marco Island, and Port32 Cape Coral amenities and services.

## Port32 Naples
- **Location**: Naples Bay area, close to Gordon Pass
- **Services**: Valet boat storage, launch and retrieval, wash/flush after every use
- **Location advantage**: 5 minutes to Gordon Pass and Gulf access, 15 minutes to Keewaydin Island, gateway to 10,000 Islands
- **Storage**: Dry storage for boats up to ~40', indoor protection from weather
- **Access**: Quick Gulf access via Gordon Pass, protected Naples Bay launching
- **Tech**: Port32 app for scheduling launches, tracking service
- **Culture**: Upscale boating community, fishing and Gulf access focused
- **Local attractions**: Historic Naples, 5th Avenue dining, world-class beaches

## Port32 Marco Island  
- **Location**: Marco Island area, gateway to Everglades
- **Services**: Valet boat storage, launch and retrieval, wash/flush after every use
- **Location advantage**: Direct access to 10,000 Islands National Wildlife Refuge, Everglades National Park backcountry
- **Storage**: Dry storage, protected from weather and storms  
- **Access**: Caxambas Pass to Gulf, extensive backcountry exploration
- **Culture**: Backcountry fishing paradise, wilderness adventure base
- **Local attractions**: Marco Island beaches, island dining, stone crab capital nearby (Goodland)

## Port32 Cape Coral
- **Location**: Caloosahatchee River, access to Charlotte Harbor
- **Services**: Valet boat storage, launch and retrieval, wash/flush after every use
- **Location advantage**: Best access to Charlotte Harbor, Pine Island Sound, Boca Grande Pass (tarpon fishing mecca)
- **Storage**: Dry storage for various boat sizes
- **Access**: River to Charlotte Harbor, Pine Island Sound shallow water paradise
- **Culture**: Mix of river cruising and flats fishing, tarpon fishing headquarters
- **Local attractions**: Cape Coral canal system, historic downtown Fort Myers nearby

## Shared SW Florida Advantages

### Weather Protection
- **Dry storage** protects from intense Florida sun, afternoon thunderstorms
