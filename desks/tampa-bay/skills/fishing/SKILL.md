# Fishing — Tampa Bay

Write fishing content cards for Tampa Bay captains at Port32 Tierra Verde and Port32 Tampa.

## Local Species & Seasons

### Year-Round
- **Snook** — passes, mangrove shorelines, dock lights. Catch-and-release June 1–Aug 31. Slot 28"–33"
- **Redfish** — grass flats, oyster bars, mangrove edges. Slot 18"–27", 1 per person
- **Spotted Seatrout** — grass flats, sandy potholes. 15"–20" slot, 3 per person
- **Sheepshead** — bridge pilings, docks, rock piles. Peak Dec–Mar. 12" min, 8 per person
- **Black Drum** — same structure as sheepshead. Peak winter

### Seasonal
- **Tarpon** (May–Aug) — Egmont Key, Skyway Bridge, passes. Catch-and-release only in state waters
- **Kingfish** (Oct–Apr) — Egmont Key reef, Skyway artificial reef
- **Cobia** (Mar–May) — follow rays on flats, Skyway area
- **Mangrove Snapper** (year-round, peak summer) — docks, bridges, nearshore reefs
- **Grouper/Snapper offshore** (Jan–May open for red grouper, check regs annually)

## Key Spots
- **Bunces Pass** — snook, redfish, trout on the flats. Watch draft at low tide.
- **Skyway Bridge** — sheepshead (winter), snook under lights (summer), kingfish nearby
- **Egmont Key** — tarpon alley (summer), snook on the beach, snapper on the reef
- **Fort De Soto flats** — wade fishing for trout and redfish
- **Weedon Island** — mangrove redfish, snook
- **Shell Key flats** — trout, redfish on low-incoming tides

## Port32 Tie-ins
- Tierra Verde puts you 5 minutes from Bunces Pass and Shell Key
- Tampa's fuel dock (Rec 90) is ideal for topping off before offshore runs
- Valet launch means you're fishing by sunrise, not trailering

## Card Types
- **Weekly fishing report** — what's biting where, based on real conditions (check recent reports)
- **Species spotlight** — deep dive on one species, local techniques, seasonal patterns
- **Spot guide** — how to fish a specific location (tide, structure, approach)
- **Seasonal transition** — what's changing, what to target next

## Research Before Writing
Always check current conditions:
- Tampa Bay fishing forums (thehulltruth.com)
- FWC fishing reports
- Local charter captain reports
- NOAA water temp and tide data for Tampa Bay
