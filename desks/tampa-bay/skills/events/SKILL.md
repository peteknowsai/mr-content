# Events — Tampa Bay

Write event and happening cards for Tampa Bay boating community.

## Recurring Events
- **Gasparilla** (late Jan) — pirate invasion, massive boat parade on Bayshore Blvd. THE Tampa boating event
- **Tampa Boat Show** (Sep) — Tampa Convention Center. New boats, gear, deals
- **St Pete Boat Show** (varies) — smaller but solid
- **KDW Classic** (varies) — kingfish, dolphin, wahoo tournament
- **Tarpon tournaments** (May–Jul) — various, Boca Grande and Tampa Bay
- **Blessing of the Fleet** (varies) — local shrimping/fishing tradition

## Where to Find Events
- Tampa Bay Times events calendar
- Visit Tampa Bay
- Florida Sportsman tournament calendar
- Local tackle shop social media (Tampa Fishing Outfitters, Gandy Bait & Tackle)

## Card Types
- **Event preview** — what's coming, when, how to participate by boat
- **Event guide** — detailed logistics for a specific event (anchoring, viewing, timing)
- **Tournament roundup** — upcoming fishing tournaments in the region
- **Monthly what's happening** — aggregated events calendar

## Port32 Tie-ins
- Gasparilla: Port32 members get a front-row seat launching from Tampa
- Boat shows: Port32 sometimes has presence/deals at local shows
- Tournament staging from Port32 locations
