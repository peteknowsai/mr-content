# Port32 — Tampa Bay

Write cards that showcase Port32 Tierra Verde and Port32 Tampa amenities and services.

## Port32 Tierra Verde
- **Address**: 200 Madonna Blvd, Tierra Verde, FL
- **Services**: Valet boat storage, launch and retrieval, wash/flush after every use
- **Location advantage**: Gateway to Gulf of Mexico. 5 min to Bunces Pass, 10 min to Shell Key, 25 min to Egmont Key
- **Storage**: Indoor dry storage for boats up to ~36'
- **Tech**: Port32 app for scheduling launches, tracking service history
- **Culture**: Serious boaters. Fishing-forward. Early morning launches common

## Port32 Tampa
- **Address**: 5200 W Tyson Ave, Tampa, FL
- **Services**: Dry storage (up to 42'), wet slips (up to 110'), fuel dock with ethanol-free Rec 90
- **On-site dining**: Hula Bay Club (Polynesian-inspired waterfront), Duke's Retired Surfer's Island Bar
- **Gulfstream Boat Club**: Boat club membership program
- **Ship store**: Basic supplies and gear
- **Valet service**: Full wash, flush, return to storage
- **Culture**: More lifestyle/social. After-work sunset runs. Hula Bay is the social hub

## Card Types
- **First-timer's guide** — what new members need to know about their marina
- **Pro tips** — how to get the most out of Port32 services (scheduling, fueling, storage)
- **Seasonal prep** — how Port32 helps with hurricane prep, winterization, spring commissioning
- **Amenity spotlight** — deep dive on one service (fuel dock, valet, app features)
- **New member welcome** — what to expect your first week

## Tone
These should feel helpful, not salesy. A captain reading this should think "oh that's useful to know" not "this is an ad." Naturally weave Port32 into the boating experience.

## Key Messages (Subtle, Not Forced)
- Valet launch saves time — you're on the water faster
- Wash/flush after every use protects your investment
- Rec 90 fuel matters for marine engines
- Indoor storage = protection from sun, storms, theft
- The app makes scheduling effortless
