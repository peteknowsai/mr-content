# Fishing — Crystal Coast

Write fishing content cards for Crystal Coast captains at Port32 Morehead City.

## Local Species & Seasons

### Year-Round
- **Red Drum** — sound flats, inlet mouths, surf zone. 18"–27" slot, 1 per person. Peak spring/fall
- **Speckled Trout** — grass flats, creek mouths, sound waters. 14" min, 4 per person
- **Flounder** — inlet areas, sound structure, nearshore ledges. 15" min
- **Black Drum** — sound flats, nearshore structure. Peak winter/spring
- **Sheepshead** — docks, pilings, nearshore structure. Peak winter/spring

### Seasonal Highlights
- **False Albacore** (Sep–Nov) — beach runs, Cape Lookout area. Famous fall fishery
- **King Mackerel** (May–Oct) — nearshore reefs, Gulf Stream edge. 24" min
- **Cobia** (May–Sep) — following rays, nearshore structure, buoys
- **Spanish Mackerel** (Apr–Nov) — inshore waters, beach areas
- **Bluefin Tuna** (Dec–Mar) — offshore, Gulf Stream. Charter/experienced captains
- **Blue Marlin** (May–Sep) — Gulf Stream, 50+ miles offshore
- **Dolphin (Mahi)** (May–Oct) — Gulf Stream, weed lines, temperature breaks

### Inshore Specialties
- **Puppy Drum** — juvenile red drum in shallow sound waters
- **Gray Trout** — deeper sound waters, winter fishery
- **Bluefish** — seasonal runs, beach and inlet areas
- **Tarpon** — summer visitors, inlet areas (smaller than Florida tarpon)

## Key Spots
- **Beaufort Inlet** — red drum, flounder, Spanish mackerel. Main offshore access
- **Cape Lookout** — false albacore, king mackerel, cobia around the point
- **Shackleford Banks** — surf fishing for drum, false albacore in fall
- **Bogue Sound** — speckled trout, puppy drum on grass flats
- **Core Sound** — trophy red drum, speckled trout
- **Newport River** — protected water fishing, speckled trout
- **Nearshore Ledges** — (8-12 miles) king mackerel, cobia, Spanish mackerel
- **Gulf Stream** — (30+ miles) bluefin, blue marlin, dolphin, wahoo

## Regulations (NC-Specific)
- **NC Fishing License** required (different from Florida!)
- **Red Drum**: 18"–27" slot, 1 per person
- **Speckled Trout**: 14" min, 4 per person  
- **Flounder**: 15" min, 4 per person
- **King Mackerel**: 24" min, 3 per person
- **Bluefin Tuna**: Federal permits required, size/bag limits change annually

## Port32 Tie-ins
- Morehead City puts you minutes from Beaufort Inlet and sound fishing
- Historic waterfront location in the heart of Crystal Coast fishing culture
- Easy access to both inshore sound fishing and offshore Gulf Stream runs
- Fuel and provisions downtown before inlet runs
