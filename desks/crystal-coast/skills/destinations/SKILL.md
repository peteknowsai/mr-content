# Destinations — Crystal Coast

Write destination and day trip cards for Crystal Coast captains exploring by boat.

## Signature Destinations

### Cape Lookout National Seashore
- **The Point** — Historic lighthouse (1859), pristine beaches, excellent fishing
- **Shackleford Banks** — Famous wild horses! Free-roaming population since 1500s
- **No Development** — Pure barrier island experience, pack everything you need
- **Access** — Boat only, protected anchorage areas marked
- **Activities** — Fishing, shelling, wildlife viewing, lighthouse climbing

### Rachel Carson Reserve  
- **Carrot Island** — Wild horses visible from Beaufort waterfront
- **Bird Watching** — Major migratory bird habitat
- **Kayaking** — Shallow waters perfect for exploration
- **Research Reserve** — Protected area, stay on designated trails

### Fort Macon State Park
- **Civil War Fort** — Well-preserved 1800s fortification
- **Beach Access** — Atlantic Beach area, good surf fishing
- **History** — Confederate fort, guided tours available
- **Facilities** — Restrooms, parking, maintained by state park

## Historic Towns by Water

### Beaufort (BOH-fort)
- **Third oldest town in NC** — Founded 1709, authentic colonial architecture
- **Waterfront boardwalk** — Restaurants, shops, maritime museum
- **Anchorage** — Good holding in Taylor Creek, dinghy to waterfront
- **History** — Blackbeard connection, maritime heritage

### Oriental  
- **Sailing Capital of NC** — Deep harbor, sailing community
- **ICW Stop** — Popular with cruisers heading north/south
- **Restaurants** — M&M's Cafe, Bean & Barrel Coffee House
- **Quiet** — Less touristy than Beaufort

## Fishing/Activity Destinations

### Cape Lookout Shoals
- **Famous Fishing** — King mackerel, cobia, false albacore
- **Gulf Stream Access** — Closer to stream than most NC locations
- **Navigation** — Shoal areas, use charts and GPS
- **Weather Dependent** — Exposed location, check conditions

### Core Sound
- **Shallow Water Paradise** — Grass flats, speckled trout, puppy drum
- **Protected Waters** — Good when ocean is rough
- **Wild Areas** — Undeveloped shoreline, pristine fishing
- **Access** — From Beaufort Inlet through Core Sound

### Bogue Banks
- **Emerald Isle** — Family beach town, fishing pier
- **Atlantic Beach** — Fort Macon area, developed beachfront
- **Indian Beach** — Quieter section, good surf fishing
- **Beach Access** — Anchor offshore, dinghy to beach

## Day Trip Planning

### Half Day (3-4 hours)
- Beaufort waterfront exploration
- Rachel Carson Reserve/Carrot Island
- Bogue Sound fishing and swimming

### Full Day (6-8 hours)
- Cape Lookout lighthouse and Shackleford Banks
- Core Sound fishing expedition  
- Fort Macon and Atlantic Beach

### Overnight Options
- **Cape Lookout** — Primitive camping with permits
- **Oriental Harbor** — Marina facilities, town exploration
- **Beaufort** — Anchor Taylor Creek, explore historic town

## Navigation Notes
- **Chart Requirements** — NOAA charts essential for shoal areas
- **Tide Considerations** — Many areas shallow at low tide
- **Weather Awareness** — Exposed waters, check marine forecast
- **No Wake Zones** — Wildlife areas and town waterfronts
