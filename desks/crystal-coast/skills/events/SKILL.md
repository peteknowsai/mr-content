# Events — Crystal Coast

Write event and tournament cards for Crystal Coast captains and boating enthusiasts.

## Signature Events

### Big Rock Blue Marlin Tournament
- **When** — Mid-June, typically Father's Day week
- **Location** — Morehead City waterfront, weigh-in at Big Rock Landing
- **Significance** — One of the East Coast's premier billfish tournaments
- **Prize Money** — Often exceeds $1 million total purse
- **Atmosphere** — Massive crowds, carnival atmosphere, live entertainment
- **Boat Access** — Spectator boats welcome, respect tournament fleet

### Crystal Coast Civic Center Events
- **Boat Shows** — Spring boat show featuring new models and marine gear
- **Fishing Seminars** — Winter seminar series with noted captains and guides
- **Saltwater Fishing Expo** — Tackle, gear, and charter boat showcase

### North Carolina Maritime Museum Events
- **Blackbeard Festival** — Annual pirate festival in Beaufort
- **Wooden Boat Show** — Traditional craft display and demonstrations
- **Maritime Heritage Programs** — Living history demonstrations

## Fishing Tournaments

### King Mackerel Tournaments
- **Crystal Coast King Mackerel Tournament** — Fall tournament
- **Various Club Tournaments** — Throughout king mackerel season (May-October)
- **Weigh-ins** — Often at downtown Morehead City waterfront

### Inshore Tournaments
- **Red Drum Tournaments** — Fall and spring events
- **Speckled Trout Tournaments** — Sound fishing competitions
- **Flounder Tournaments** — Summer flatfish competitions

### Offshore Tournaments
- **Blue Marlin Tournaments** — Summer billfish events
- **Dolphin Tournaments** — Mahi-mahi competitions
- **Wahoo Tournaments** — Winter high-speed trolling events

## Seasonal Event Calendar

### Spring (March-May)
- **Boat Shows** — New season kickoff events
- **Fishing Seminars** — Pre-season strategy sessions
- **Marina Open Houses** — Tour facilities, meet staff

### Summer (June-August)
- **Big Rock Blue Marlin Tournament** — The big event
- **Beach Music Festivals** — Carolina beach music and dancing
- **Historic Home Tours** — Beaufort historic district

### Fall (September-November)
- **False Albacore Tournaments** — Celebrating the fall run
- **Seafood Festivals** — Celebrating local harvest
- **Hurricane Relief Events** — Community support efforts when needed

### Winter (December-February)
- **Bluefin Tournaments** — Cold water offshore events
- **Holiday Boat Parades** — Lighted boat parades
- **Fishing Seminars** — Indoor programs during slow season
