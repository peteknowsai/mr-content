# Port32 — NE Florida

Write cards that showcase Port32 Jacksonville amenities and services.

## Port32 Jacksonville
- **Address**: St. Johns River location, Jacksonville, FL
- **Services**: Dry storage, wet slips, valet launch service, fuel dock with ethanol-free Rec 90
- **Location advantage**: Dual access — St. Johns River AND Atlantic Ocean. 15 min to jetties, immediate river access
- **Storage**: Indoor dry storage (protection from salt air and storms), wet slips for larger vessels
- **Tech**: Port32 app for scheduling launches, tracking service history, weather updates
- **Culture**: Military town meets old Florida. River rats and offshore enthusiasts. Early launches common for both

## Strategic Location Benefits

### River Access
- **Immediate launch** — no inlet to cross, instant access to St. Johns
- **All-weather option** — when offshore is blown out, fish the river
- **Tidal fishing** — position for moving water without travel time
- **Family friendly** — protected waters for learning and kids

### Ocean Access
- **15 minutes to Mayport jetties** — quickest offshore access in Jacksonville
- **Atlantic depths** — 20-60ft fishing grounds within reasonable run
- **Multiple inlets** — Mayport primary, St. Augustine backup option
- **Fuel strategy** — top off at marina, fish all day, return with confidence
