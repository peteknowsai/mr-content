# Events — NE Florida

Write event and tournament cards for First Coast boating community.

## Annual Fishing Tournaments

### Greater Jacksonville Kingfish Tournament
- **When**: Typically October (fall king mackerel season)
- **Where**: Mayport area, weigh-in at marina
- **What**: King mackerel tournament, multiple divisions
- **Why it matters**: Largest local kingfish tournament, serious money and prizes
- **Port32 advantage**: 15 minutes to fishing grounds, early launch capability

### Mayport Redfish Tournament
- **When**: Fall/winter (peak redfish season)
- **Where**: St. Johns River and Nassau Sound
- **Format**: Catch/photo/release typically
- **Categories**: Multiple boat classes, youth division
- **Local knowledge**: River specialists have advantage

### Amelia Island Tournament Series
- **Season**: Spring through fall
- **Species**: Multi-species tournaments
- **Format**: Various formats throughout year
- **Location**: Nassau Sound, offshore, river options
- **Family focus**: Many events include youth categories

## Seasonal Events

### Fall Events (Sep-Nov)

- **Military family events** — back-to-school and deployment cycle events

### Winter Events (Dec-Feb)

- **Planning seminars** — boat maintenance, fishing technique workshops
- **Military family support** — holiday events during deployment season

## Event Planning Considerations

### Marina Impact
- **Increased traffic** — plan fuel and launch times accordingly
- **Slip availability** — reserve early for major events
- **Service delays** — busy periods may affect valet timing
- **Parking** — land-side parking fills during major events

### Weather Contingencies
- **Hurricane season** — Sept-Nov events may face cancellations
- **Winter weather** — nor'easters can disrupt December-February events
- **Summer storms** — afternoon thunderstorms affect evening events
- **Military exercises** — can affect offshore tournament areas
