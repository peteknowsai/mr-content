# Dining — Southeast Florida

Write dock-and-dine and waterfront restaurant cards for SE Florida captains.

## Dock-and-Dine from Fort Lauderdale

### New River
- **Shooters Waterfront** — Popular with yacht crews. Dock space available. American fare, full bar
- **Coconuts** — New River. Casual waterfront, live music. Good dock space for day boats
- **Kaluz Restaurant** — Las Olas. Upscale waterfront dining. Limited dock space, call ahead
- **Wild East Asian Bistro** — New River. Asian fusion, good for date night by boat

### Port Everglades Area  
- **GG's Waterfront Bar & Grill** — Hollywood. Casual, biker-friendly. Good burgers, cold beer
- **Le Tub** — Hollywood Beach. Famous burgers, quirky atmosphere. Beach access, no dock

## Dock-and-Dine from Lighthouse Point

### Hillsboro Inlet Area
- **Lighthouse Point Yacht Club** — Members and guests. Call ahead for dock space
- **Cap's Place** — Historic fish camp on Lighthouse Point. Water taxi required, worth the trip
- **Deerfield Beach Cafe** — Casual beachfront. No dock, but beach your dinghy

### ICW Access
- **Whale's Rib** — Deerfield Beach. Raw bar, seafood. Limited dock space
- **JB's on the Beach** — Deerfield Beach. Beach bar, volleyball. Anchor off the beach
- **Oceans 234** — Deerfield Beach Pier. Upscale seafood. No boat access, but worth the Uber

## Dock-and-Dine from Palm Beach Gardens

### Jupiter Area  
- **Jupiter Beach Resort** — Sinclair's Ocean Grill. Upscale beachfront dining. Call for dock availability
- **Square Grouper** — Jupiter. Tiki bar atmosphere, live music. Good dock space on ICW
- **Guanabanas** — Jupiter. Waterfront tiki bar, live music. Very popular, gets crowded
- **Jetty's Waterfront Restaurant** — Jupiter Inlet. Right at the inlet, watch boats come and go

### Loxahatchee River
- **Riverwalk Cafe** — Jupiter. Casual riverside dining. Good dock space, manatee watching
- **Food Shack** — Jupiter. Casual riverside, local favorite. Limited dock space

## By Boat (Worth the Run)

### North (from Palm Beach Gardens)
- **Stuart Yacht Club** — Stuart. Reciprocal privileges, call ahead
- **Manatee Pocket** — Port Salerno. Multiple restaurants, good hurricane hole

### South (from Fort Lauderdale)  
- **Islamorada Fish Company** — Bass Pro Shops, Dania Beach. No direct boat access but close to marina
- **Bahia Mar** — Fort Lauderdale Beach. Multiple restaurants, transient slips available

## Bahamas (For Extended Cruising)
- **Atlantis Marina Village** — Paradise Island. Full service, multiple restaurants
- **Nassau Yacht Haven** — Nassau Harbor. Downtown Nassau dining access
- **Compass Point** — West Bay. Beachfront dining, limited dock space

## Card Types
- **Restaurant spotlight** — one restaurant, how to get there by boat, dock info, what to order
- **Dock-and-dine roundup** — best options from a specific marina area  
- **Date night by boat** — upscale waterfront options in SE Florida
- **Post-fishing grub** — casual spots to grab food after offshore trips
- **Bahamas dining** — restaurant options for extended cruising

## Port32 Tie-ins
- Fort Lauderdale: Close to New River dining, yacht-friendly restaurants
- Lighthouse Point: Heart of sportfishing area, casual post-tournament spots  
- Palm Beach Gardens: Jupiter dining scene, gateway to Bahamas restaurant runs

## Research Notes
- Always verify dock availability and approach depths
- Check restaurant hours and seasonal closures
- Note fuel dock locations near popular dining areas
- ICW restaurants often have shallow approaches — check your draft
