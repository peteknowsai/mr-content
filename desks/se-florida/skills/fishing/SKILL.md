# Fishing — Southeast Florida

Write fishing content cards for SE Florida captains at Port32 Fort Lauderdale, Lighthouse Point, and Palm Beach Gardens.

## Local Species & Seasons

### Offshore (Gulf Stream/Deep Water)
- **Sailfish** — Dec-Apr peak. Kite fishing and trolling. Catch-and-release only. Sailfish Alley off Lighthouse Point
- **Mahi-mahi** — Apr-Aug peak when Gulf Stream is closest. Trolling and live bait
- **Wahoo** — Nov-Mar peak. High-speed trolling, wire leader essential
- **Blackfin Tuna** — Year-round. Peak winter months. Chunk bait and trolling
- **King Mackerel** — Oct-Mar. Slow trolling live bait, downriggers
- **Swordfish** — Year-round. Deep dropping at night, 1200-1800 ft

### Nearshore (5-30 miles)
- **Cobia** — Mar-May peak. Follow rays, live bait
- **Kingfish** — Oct-Apr peak. Slow trolling and live bait
- **Bonito** — Dec-Mar. Small trolling lures and spoons
- **Barracuda** — Year-round. Wire leader, strip baits
- **Amberjack** — Year-round on wrecks and reefs. Heavy tackle required

### Inshore/Backcountry
- **Snook** — Loxahatchee River, New River, ICW docks. Slot 28"-33", closed June-Aug
- **Tarpon** — May-Jul peak. ICW and inlet mouths. Catch-and-release in state waters
- **Redfish** — Loxahatchee River mangroves. Slot 18"-27"
- **Spotted Seatrout** — Grass flats, potholes. 15"-20" slot
- **Sheepshead** — Dock pilings, bridges. Peak Dec-Mar

## Key Spots

### Offshore
- **Sailfish Alley** — 120-200 ft depths off Lighthouse Point. Prime sailfish grounds
- **Gulf Stream Edge** — 3-5 miles offshore. Blue water species
- **Jupiter Ledge** — Natural reef system off Jupiter. Grouper, snapper, cobia
- **Fort Lauderdale Reef Tract** — Scattered patch reefs, kingfish territory

### Nearshore  
- **Hillsboro Inlet mouth** — Tarpon in summer, snook year-round
- **Jupiter Inlet** — Snook on the rocks, tarpon in the inlet
- **Port Everglades** — Kings and cobia on the outgoing tide

### Inshore
- **Loxahatchee River** — Snook and tarpon in the mangroves
- **New River** — Urban snook fishing, dock lights at night
- **ICW from Jupiter to Fort Lauderdale** — Snook, tarpon, jacks

## Port32 Tie-ins
- Fort Lauderdale: Full fuel, bait, and 150-ton lift for refit between fishing seasons
- Lighthouse Point: Heart of Sailfish Alley — 20 minutes to the blue water
- Palm Beach Gardens: Jupiter Inlet access, perfect for Bahamas fishing trips

## Card Types
- **Weekly fishing report** — what's biting where, based on real Gulf Stream conditions
- **Species spotlight** — sailfish kite fishing, mahi trolling patterns, swordfish deep dropping
- **Spot guide** — how to fish Jupiter Ledge, Sailfish Alley techniques
- **Seasonal transition** — when sailfish arrive, mahi migration patterns

## Research Before Writing
Always check current conditions:
- Sailfish Marina fishing reports (Palm Beach)
- South Florida charter captain reports
- Gulf Stream position and temperature (NOAA)
- NOAA marine weather for offshore zones
- FWC Southeast region fishing reports
