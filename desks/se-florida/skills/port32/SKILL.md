# Port32 — Southeast Florida

Write marina guide and amenity cards for Port32 Fort Lauderdale, Lighthouse Point, and Palm Beach Gardens.

## Port32 Fort Lauderdale

### Location & Access
- **Address**: New River, Fort Lauderdale
- **Access**: Deep water access via New River and Port Everglades
- **Approach**: No bridge restrictions on New River approach
- **Coordinates**: Deep draft vessels welcome

### Specialty: Yacht Services & Refit
- **150-ton travel lift** — Largest in SE Florida Port32 network
- **Full boatyard services** — Hull repair, mechanical, electrical, rigging
- **Yacht refit hub** — Winter maintenance headquarters for snowbird yachts
- **Professional contractors** — Vetted vendors for all yacht systems

### Amenities
- **Fuel dock** — Gas and diesel, competitive pricing
- **Ship's store** — Basic provisions and marine supplies
- **Pump-out service** — Available at fuel dock
- **Security** — Gated facility with 24/7 monitoring
- **Power** — 30/50 amp service, 100 amp available for larger vessels
- **WiFi** — Complimentary throughout facility

### Nearby Services
- **Marine chandleries** — Multiple shops within 5 miles
- **West Marine** — 10 minutes by car
- **Restaurants** — New River dining within walking distance
- **Airport** — Fort Lauderdale International 20 minutes

## Port32 Lighthouse Point  

### Location & Access
- **Address**: Hillsboro Inlet area, Lighthouse Point  
- **Access**: Via Hillsboro Inlet to ICW
- **Approach**: Watch tides at Hillsboro Inlet, can be rough with northeast wind
- **Draft restrictions**: Check depths at low tide

### Specialty: Sportfishing Hub
- **Sailfish Alley access** — 20 minutes to prime billfish grounds
- **Tournament support** — Weigh station and tournament services
- **Charter fleet base** — Multiple sportfishing charters operate from here
- **Fishing guides** — Inshore and offshore guide recommendations

### Amenities  
- **Fuel dock** — High-speed diesel for tournament boats
- **Ice and bait** — Live and frozen bait available
- **Cleaning station** — Fish cleaning with disposal
- **Tackle shop** — Offshore tackle and local knowledge
- **Boat ramp** — Trailer boat access
- **Tournament staging** — Facilities for fishing tournaments

### Fishing Intelligence
- **Local captains** — Daily fishing reports and conditions
- **Bait sources** — Live ballyhoo, pilchards, goggle eyes
- **Weather window** — Best local intel for offshore conditions

## Port32 Palm Beach Gardens

### Location & Access
- **Address**: Jupiter area, access via Jupiter Inlet
- **Access**: Jupiter Inlet to ICW and Loxahatchee River
- **Approach**: Jupiter Inlet can be tricky with northeast wind against outgoing tide
- **Bahamas gateway** — Closest Port32 marina to Bahamas

### Specialty: Bahamas Cruising Base  
- **Bahamas runs** — 50 miles to Bimini, 60 miles to Grand Bahama
- **Customs clearance** — Documentation and customs guidance
- **Extended stays** — Monthly rates for Bahamas cruisers
- **Provisioning** — Stock up before international trips

### Amenities
- **Fuel dock** — Gas and diesel, top off before ocean crossings  
- **Ship's store** — Provisioning for extended cruises
- **Pump-out** — Required before Bahamas entry
- **Customs support** — Help with documentation and procedures
- **Concierge services** — Trip planning assistance
- **Loxahatchee River access** — Scenic river cruising

### Bahamas Support Services
- **Trip planning** — Route recommendations and weather windows
- **Documentation check** — Ensure proper paperwork
- **Fuel planning** — Calculate range and fuel stops
- **Equipment check** — Safety gear requirements for international waters
